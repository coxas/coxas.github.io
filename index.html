I'm a graduate student at the University of Chicago taking classes in web development, mobile computing, and software engineering. I believe that good design is essential to obtaining success in today's visually driven world.

I am studying computer science and public policy through the Harris School's MSCAPP program. So far, I've taken classes in object-oriented programming, databases, machine learning, and web development. This year I plan to dive deeper into advanced web development, Swift programming, iOS, and software engineering.

Though I haven't always known it, I'm truly an artist at heart. Visual design is my passion and I'm fascinated by the way design impacts those who interact with it. Human-Computer Interaction is an area of research I would like to pursue in the future.
